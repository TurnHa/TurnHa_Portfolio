mugford street players

mugford street players is a theatre group that started in 1975 in marblehead massachusetts. they perform across the states and internationally. the work i have here was a class assignment from college that was aimed to test my branding skills. for this group i have created different sets of logos, selected the brand colors, and created print materials for their upcoming show "a picasso".
