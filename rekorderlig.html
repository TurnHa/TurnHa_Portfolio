rekorderlig package re-design

this was another college class assignment i had in 2017. we were challenged to take a product and add an extension of a flavor or concept to it. so i went with rekorderlig - this is a hard cider brand known for it's fruity flavors, and unique illustration style on the packaging. so i created this mango and lime flavored cider label and modeled it after the original rekorderlig label. with this project i explored various fonts, and colors until i achieved the perfect design that looked similar to the other flavors from rekorderlig.
